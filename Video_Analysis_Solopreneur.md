## Key Observations

### 1. The $10M Foundation
| Aspect | Insight for $1B Goal |
|--------|---------------------|
| Revenue Target | $10M/year = ~$833K/month = $27K/day |
| Business Model | Need scalable, repeatable systems |
| Team Building | Hire specialists, not generalists |
| Focus | Deep focus on one channel first |

### 2. The Playbook Structure
Based on successful founder patterns:

#### Phase 1: Foundation ($0-$100K)
- [ ] Identify proven demand
- [ ] Create minimum viable offer
- [ ] Get first 10 paying customers
- [ ] Validate pricing

#### Phase 2: Scale ($100K-$1M)
- [ ] Systemize sales process
- [ ] Build team (even if small)
- [ ] Optimize conversion rates
- [ ] Add additional revenue streams

#### Phase 3: Growth ($1M-$10M)
- [ ] Expand team capabilities
- [ ] Multiple marketing channels
- [ ] Automation of repetitive tasks
- [ ] Standardize operations

#### Phase 4: Scale to $1B
- [ ] Leverage AI agents (this is where OpenClaw fits!)
- [ ] Build moats & network effects
- [ ] Consider acquisitions
- [ ] Scale internationally
